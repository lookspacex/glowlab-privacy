In the current version:

- We **do not require you to create an account or sign in**;  
- We **do not access or upload** your photos, camera roll, contacts, messages, or any personal content;  
- We **do not** intentionally collect your name, email address, phone number, or other personally identifiable information, unless you explicitly provide it when contacting us by email.

- 应用版本号  
- 匿名的使用信息（例如功能使用频率、错误信息）

To ensure the normal operation of the App, improve the user experience, and troubleshoot issues, we may automatically receive and log certain technical information on our servers, including but not limited to:

- Device model (e.g., iPhone 14 Pro)  
- OS version (e.g., iOS 18.x)  
- App version  
- Anonymous usage information (such as feature usage frequency, error messages)  
- IP address and request time (automatically logged by servers for security and anti-abuse purposes)

这些信息主要以**汇总或匿名形式**使用，不会用于识别您个人身份。

This information is mainly used in **aggregated or anonymized form** and is not used to directly identify you as an individual.
